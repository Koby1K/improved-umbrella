There is one way in particular I would clean up this UX. When you click on "tap to add" a sauce or topping and you have the option for light, normal, or extra but, you have to use the + and - icons to cycle through options. Instead I would save the user a few clicks by displaying all three options at the same time on the UI as buttons that the user can just simply click on one and move on to the next objective.


What Amount of sauce would you like?

This design will make for a cleaner look within the mobile app as to many words on a small screen can make the layout feel clustered and unappealing. With that being said also changing buttons that cycle thru option such as the - light,normal,extra + button It would be more efficient and easier for the user if all three options were made into buttons that could all be seen and chosen at the same time.
