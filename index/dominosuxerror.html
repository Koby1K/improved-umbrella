Everone loves pizza, Domino's is one of the top pizza chains in the world and even revolutionized online orders for pizza.


What if I told you that Dominos could improve its UX by doing one simple steps that could lead to customer retaining and an even larger market share because they saved a few clicks.



As you can see,this UI showcases an inefficient use of the "Tap to add and Tap to Remove buttons" when you choose one of those options it then brings another option under it for the amount of sauce or topping you would like.


However, In addition to this when you click "TAP TO ADD" another input for light, normal, or extra toppings or sauce between + and - buttons. It would be faster and more efficient to display all three choices as buttons at the same time so the user can just click on one
