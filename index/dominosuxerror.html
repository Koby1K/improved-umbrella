Everone loves pizza, Domino's is one of the top pizza chains in the world and even revolutionized online orders for pizza.


What if I told you that Dominos could improve its UX by doing a couple of simple steps that could lead to customer retaining and an even larger market share.



As you can see,this UI showcases an redudant use of "TAP TO ADD" buttons these can be replaced with radio selectors for a cleaner layout. In addition to this when you click "TAP TO ADD" another input for light, normal, or extra toppings or sauce between + and - buttons. It would be faster and more efficient to display all three choices as buttons at the same time so the user can just click on one
